About US

The idea for MindSpark was born during a late-night conversation between friends who struggled with staying motivated and focused in their academic and creative pursuits. Realizing how many students faced the same problem, we wanted to create a solution that combines productivity tools with mental well-being. Our team of classmates came together, each bringing a unique skillset—from design to development to psychology. We believe that small daily sparks of motivation can lead to big transformations. That’s why MindSpark exists—to help students unlock their full potential.

Mission: To empower students with intelligent tools that blend productivity and wellness for sustainable success.
